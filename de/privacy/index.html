Privacy Policy for BikeApp

Effective date: April 6, 2025

BikeApp is a smartwatch application developed for Wear OS and Garmin Connect IQ. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience.

1. Data Collection

BikeApp does not collect any personal data such as name, email address, or location.

The only data we collect is the device ID, which is used solely for payment processing via PayPal.
We do not create user accounts or store any additional user-related information.

2. Payments

Payments within BikeApp are processed via PayPal.
All payment operations are handled securely by PayPal in accordance with their privacy policy.
BikeApp does not store any credit card details or PayPal login credentials.

3. Third-Party Services

Other than PayPal for payment processing, the app does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertisements.

4. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. The device ID we collect is not shared with third parties and is only used to confirm payment transactions.

BikeApp is provided “as is”, without warranties of any kind.
We do not guarantee that the app will be free of errors or that it will function without interruptions.
We are not responsible for any loss, damage, or inconvenience caused by the use of the app, including but not limited to app malfunctions or service unavailability.
